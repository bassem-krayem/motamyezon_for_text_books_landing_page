Features

Accessible Book Uploads

Admins can upload books in accessible formats (EPUB, KFX, AZW3) to make them available for blind users, ensuring a better reading experience compared to PDFs.

Book Details and Downloads

Each book listing includes the title, author, description, and download links for three accessible formats, making it easy for users to find and access books.

Organized Book Categories

Admins can add categories for books, allowing users to browse by topics like Fiction, Science, or History. Users can select a category from a dropdown menu to view only the books in that category, simplifying navigation.

Admin Management Tools

Admins have the ability to delete and update book listings, ensuring that the library remains current and relevant.

About

Motamyezon for Text Books was inspired by the dedicated volunteer team at Motamyezon, who spend their time converting books into accessible formats for blind readers. Their work transforms PDF files into EPUB, KFX, and AZW3 formats, making reading accessible for everyone in the community. Books are organized by categories, making it easy for users to find topics that interest them.

Having read many books from their library, I wanted to give back by providing this free website to support their work and make it easier than publishing on Telegram.
